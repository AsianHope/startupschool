Summer Startup School
hosted by Logos International School

From June 29 - July 10 2015, Logos International School is hosting its annual summer school program. While many Logos students will be in attendance, these programs are open to anyone. More details atthe Logos website: logoscambodia.org

The cost of this two week course is $120 for current Logos students, $150 for students of other schools.

About Summer Startup School

Open to Students Entering Grade 8-12 (Grade 6/7 with special permission)

Description:

Begin learning the engineering skills needed to build a technology startup from the ground up. This isn't just a 'make your own web page', but a deep look at the technologies behind today's successful tech businesses.

We'll be looking at things like:

  • HTML/CSS/JS
  • Frameworks (emphasizing AngularJS)
  • Building a single page application (SPA)
  • Working with a REST API
  • Virtual Machines, IAAS, Unix/Linux command line, text editors
  • Version control with git

It's not just tech stuff though! Along the way we'll also be taking a broad look at some principles of starting a small business.

  • Choosing your customer
  • Case Studies of existing companies
  • Developing a business model
  • Performing a feasability study
  • Finding funding

If none of that makes any sense, that's okay! We'll cover it all and more. Beginners are encouraged

A word of warning:
This will be a rapidly paced course where students will be expected to be responsible for their own learning.

You will, at times, be:

  • confused
  • frustrated
  • tired

If you want a better understanding of what lies ahead, you should definitely read: Why Learning to Code is So Damn Hard

Logos High School Students with exceptional final products and demonstrable learning may receive IT credit for this course. Please see Mr. Kozloff if you want to take this course for credit.

Requirements:

  • Types at a decent pace (45 WPM++)
  • Reads and understands English reasonably well
  • Comfortable trying new things independently
  • Has taken Codeacademy's Free Make a Website course (or is comfortable in HTML/CSS)

Even-better-ifs:

  • Has taken a programming class (At Logos: MS Computers, PnD, APCS, Robots)
  • Is an independent learner
  • Able/willing to put in extra time at home

About Logos International School

Logos International School is a ministry of the not-for-profit, international non-governmental organization Asian Hope Cambodia and is located in Phnom Penh Thmei, a northwest suburb in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. Logos is a fully accredited member of both the Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI) and the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC). Most of the teachers at Logos come from North America but other countries are represented, as well. Cambodian teacher’s aides assist in the classroom and are mentored by the classroom teacher at the elementary level. Currently, Logos serves over 350 students from pre-school to grade 12. The average class size is twenty-five students per grade.

About Asian Hope

Founded in 1999, Asian Hope is an international, Christian nonprofit organization dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children in Cambodia. Asian Hope’s passion is to dramatically alter the future of impoverished children in Cambodia through life-changing programs of Christian education and community development.

Asian Hope believes that effective and lasting community transformation comes through equipping future generations to be the catalysts of hope and change. Asian Hope currently protects, educates and empowers more than 1,600 children in Cambodia. To learn more about Asian Hope, visit www.asianhope.org like us on Facebook and follow us on Twitter.
